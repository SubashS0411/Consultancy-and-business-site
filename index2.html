FAQ

Answers to common questions

Still curious? We’ll tailor specifics to your context.

How fast can we start?

Discovery begins within a week; most squads launch inside 3 weeks with aligned scope and success metrics.

Do you work with internal teams?

Yes—our squads embed with your leads, co-own KPIs, and transfer playbooks so teams keep momentum post-engagement.

How do you track value?

We define a KPI stack, baseline it, and report weekly with variance bands, risks, and unblockers, all visible in your dashboard.

Ready to move from plan to proof?

Pick a starting point—strategy sprint or embedded squad—and we’ll design the first 6 weeks together.
